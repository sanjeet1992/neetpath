Quick Answer

State Wise NEET UG Counselling Websites 2026 are official government portals for MBBS and BDS admissions under State Quota, Management Quota and NRI Quota seats. Each state conducts counselling separately, while All India Quota (AIQ) counselling is done centrally by MCC at mcc.nic.in.

📝
Separate registration required on each state's official counselling portal
🏛️
85% State Quota seats allotted through respective state counselling bodies
🇮🇳
AIQ (15%) + Deemed + Central seats via MCC at mcc.nic.in
📄
Domicile / residency certificate mandatory for State Quota in most states
🌍
Management & NRI Quota seats may not require domicile — check state rules
⚠️
Incorrect portal registration can lead to disqualification from that round
📅
State counselling 2026 expected between July–August 2026
🏥
